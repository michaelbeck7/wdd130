Overview

Purpose

Think about how you keep track of all your recipes right now: is it a cookbook stored away somewhere? An overflowing folder that you have to dig through every time a holiday comes around and you need? What if you could access all of your recipes in one place from your phone, or computer. Thatâ€™s what my website would be: a single place to store all my recipes whether you're grocery shopping, cooking, or planning my meals for the week.

Audience

The target audience of this website is busy college students looking for easy recipes. They are people who enjoy cooking. Because they are busy and looking to maximize every minute, they expect a site that is simple, clean, and presents recipes without extra fluff. Becaues our audience will be using both phones and laptops, the website must be responsive and easy to navigate on both mobile and desktop.
